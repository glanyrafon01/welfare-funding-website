### **Phase 1: Pre-Consultation (Weeks 1–4)**
**Goal:** Develop a draft five-year plan and prepare for consultation.

#### **1. Draft the Five-Year Plan**
- **Key Objectives:** Replace heating, fix exterior, redesign bar area, and other improvements.
- **Action:**
  - List objectives with estimated costs, timelines, and priority levels.
  - Include a section on how improvements align with community needs (e.g., accessibility, energy efficiency).
  - Draft a simple, visual version for public consultation (e.g., infographic or one-pager).
- **Responsible:** Trustees/Working Group
- **Resources:** LibreOffice (for drafting), Canva (for visuals).

#### **2. Agree on the Plan with Trustees**
- **Action:**
  - Present draft plan at a trustee meeting.
  - Revise based on feedback and finalize.
- **Responsible:** Chair/Secretary
- **Timeline:** Week 3

#### **3. Set Up Consultation Platforms**
- **Online:**
  - Create a simple website (e.g., GitHub Pages or Vercel) to host the plan and consultation.
  - Include a feedback form (Google Forms or SurveyMonkey).
  - Generate a QR code linking to the website (use a free QR code generator).
- **Offline:**
  - Design a paper consultation form (matching the online version).
  - Print copies for distribution at the hall and local venues.
- **Responsible:** Trustee/Volunteer
- **Timeline:** Week 4

---

### **Phase 2: Consultation (Weeks 5–8)**
**Goal:** Gather community input on the five-year plan.

#### **1. Launch Consultation**
- **Action:**
  - Publish the plan on the website and social media (Facebook).
  - Distribute paper forms at the hall, local shops, and community centers.
  - Promote the consultation via:
    - Facebook posts (daily reminders).
    - Flyers with QR codes (post in the hall and local venues).
    - Local press (if possible).
- **Responsible:** Communications Trustee/Volunteer

#### **2. Community Meeting**
- **Action:**
  - Organize a meeting at the hall to present the plan and gather feedback.
  - Record comments and suggestions.
- **Responsible:** Chair/Secretary
- **Timeline:** Week 6

#### **3. Collect and Analyze Feedback**
- **Action:**
  - Collate responses from online, paper, and meeting.
  - Summarize key themes and suggestions.
  - Revise the five-year plan based on feedback.
- **Responsible:** Trustee/Working Group
- **Timeline:** Week 8

---

### **Phase 3: Grant Application (Weeks 9–12)**
**Goal:** Apply for funding (e.g., National Lottery Community Fund).

#### **1. Identify Suitable Grants**
- **Action:**
  - Research grants for community halls (e.g., [National Lottery Community Fund](https://www.tnlcommunityfund.org.uk/), [WCVA](https://wcva.cymru/), local council grants).
  - Check eligibility and deadlines.
- **Responsible:** Trustee/Volunteer

#### **2. Prepare Application**
- **Action:**
  - Use the revised five-year plan as the basis for the application.
  - Include:
    - Community consultation results (evidence of need).
    - Detailed costings and timelines.
    - Letters of support (if required).
  - Draft a compelling narrative about the hall’s role in the community.
- **Responsible:** Trustee/Working Group
- **Timeline:** Week 10

#### **3. Submit Application**
- **Action:**
  - Review application with trustees.
  - Submit before the deadline.
- **Responsible:** Chair/Secretary
- **Timeline:** Week 12

---

### **Phase 4: Implementation (Post-Funding)**
**Goal:** Deliver the plan if funding is secured.

#### **1. Project Management**
- **Action:**
  - Break the plan into phases (e.g., heating replacement first).
  - Assign trustees/volunteers to oversee each phase.
  - Set up a tracking system (spreadsheet or project management tool).
- **Responsible:** Trustee/Working Group

#### **2. Community Updates**
- **Action:**
  - Regular updates via website, Facebook, and noticeboards.
  - Celebrate milestones (e.g., completion of heating replacement).
- **Responsible:** Communications Trustee

#### **3. Compliance and Reporting**
- **Action:**
  - Keep records of all spending and progress for grant reporting.
  - Submit reports to funders as required.
- **Responsible:** Treasurer/Secretary

---

### **Compliance Notes**
- **Data Protection:** Ensure consultation responses are anonymized and stored securely (GDPR compliance).
- **Charity Commission:** If income exceeds £25k, ensure accounts are prepared and filed on time.
- **Welsh Language:** Provide bilingual consultation materials and application documents.
